Discord Bots (And Why They're So Useful)

If you use (or have used) Discord, you've probably come across one of the Discord Bots.
They have various functions, fun commands, etc. Now, why are they so used?

It''s usefulness

From what I've seen, bots are used for their moderation functions. A Server Administrator can easily
banish, mutate and kick a user who is causing problems. Discord doesn't natively offer many options,
so using bots is a big plus. The most famous bots I know are without a doubt Lorrita, Carl, MEE6, among others.


Small Bots also deserve attention

There are some small bots that, despite not being well known, are very powerful. An example
it's the Threadripper bot, from my friend s0ra. It takes great care of one of discord's new features, threads.
I'm also developing my Bot, but it would be unfair to talk about it because it's not ready yet.

Is it worth using bots?

I would say yes. Bots that have been around for a long time are quite mature. Its functions are very powerful,
and bring benefits even to the smallest servers.
