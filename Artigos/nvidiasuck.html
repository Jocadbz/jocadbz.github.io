Nvidia is the worst video card brand in history

Please do not buy Nvidia graphics cards. They can be powerful, but you'll spend
more time figuring out why it doesn't work than actually playing on it. Let's take
an example. On linux, you have to install the proprietary drivers, which is not
that difficult. But actually it is. For example, I've seen several cases of people
trying to use their video card with proprietary drivers, and after restarting the
computer, it just didn't show any video.

What about AMD cards?

Install the card, turn on the computer and use it. Just that. When we say to
prefer AMD's, and because of the quality of the software. The fact that the drivers
are open-source makes it possible to include their drivers in the repositories of
many distros, such as Debian (which I use).

Is the headache the only problem ?

Unfortunately not. Nvidia doesn't care about you. For those who play with their
video cards. To them, you're just a walking wallet with legs. They even launched
a CPU for Bitcoin mining, which shows that they only go after what makes the most
money, leaving aside, the people who USE their GPUs. In other words, we have bad
products, bad DRIVERS, and bad customer service.


If you still want to opt for an Nvidia card, that's fine, go for it. But know that you will not have a good service.
